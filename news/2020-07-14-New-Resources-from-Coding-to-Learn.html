New Co-Design Toolkit from Coding to Learn

July 14, 2020

The Coding to Learn project team has created a co-design kit aimed at engaging kids with complex disabilities in coding skills and education is being created. The growing collection of activities includes multi-modal collaborative art creation.

Since the COVID-19 isolation period, the team has explored remote coding and creativity workshops. Working with teachers, parents and students with complex disabilities, the team is developing a set of activities and processes to engage students remotely. These new insights will be added to the co-design kit in the coming weeks.

The Inclusive Coding Environment is being developed through a collaborative co-design process, currently including remote co-design coding sessions.
